Kimmie

Kimmie
Tanoi

Birthday: June 17 | Age: 19 | Species: Shapeshifting slime | Nicknames: Kibi, Gibbick | Pronouns: any | Height: 182cm (in original form)

A successful chemist from a young age,
Kimmie developed the main alternative to the Great Zapfish used
by areas not connected by Inkadian power systems using ink from Turf Wars and Anarchy/Ranked Battles, and developed significant riches from patenting the product
and monopolising all production, ensuring it is not used against his wishes.
During their research, they faced an accident in the labs which turned them into a water-based slime.
They're able to adjust their slime's entire physical and chemical composition, and they use this to make weapons they use dubiously legally in battle.

Personality

Kimmie is a playful yet kind individual, who will do almost anything to achieve his goals, as outlandish as they sometimes are.
They will always help out their friends, either in a physical or emotional aspect.
They enjoy headpats and cuddles, and is not immune to losing battles because of them.
Kimmie has a fighting spirit, which they channel into all their activities and interests.
Getting on Kimmie's bad side is not a good idea though, as they will VERY happily
use their abilties to make sure you will reconsider your actions or just kill you gleefully and smile as you suffer.

Interests

Due to their significant riches from their initial fuel development, they have been able to invest significantly in many vanity projects of his,
including a large robotics division of KTN Chemicals which is used to develop systems used in industry and day to day life.
Kimmie is also very involved in the Tableturf scene, using an alternative persona and appearing as an Inkling to avoid being spotted due to their fame.
They manage the local tournaments and provide a significant amount of funding, under a cover company to hide the fact Kimmie uses her big money to fund funny card game tournaments.
Additionally, Kimmie is a successful rally driver (winning 2 world championships) and martial arts practitioner, ensuring not to use their abilities.
